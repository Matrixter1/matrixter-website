The Truth Beneath

Before we are anything — we are one.

Before we are American or African, Christian or Muslim, English-speaking or Mandarin-speaking, rich or poor, young or old — before any of these — we are human.

Every label was added later. Every difference was layered on. Beneath them all, the same heart, the same breath, the same astonishing fact of being alive on the same small world.

The Science of Sameness

Every human you will ever meet is almost entirely you.

99.9%

The genetic code you carry is 99.9% identical to every other human alive. The thing we call “difference” is one tenth of one percent.
